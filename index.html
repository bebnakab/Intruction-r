Essential Formulas

Quick reference for the most important equations in each unit

Kinematics

v = v₀ + at
x = v₀t + ½at²
v² = v₀² + 2ax

Newton's 2nd Law

F = ma
W = mg
f = μN

Energy & Work

W = Fd·cos(θ)
KE = ½mv²
PE = mgh

Electricity

V = IR
F = kq₁q₂/r²
P = IV

Waves

v = fλ
T = 2π√(L/g)
n₁sin(θ₁) = n₂sin(θ₂)

Modern Physics

E = mc²
E = hf
ΔxΔp ≥ ℏ/2

About This Course

Welcome to Physics Learning Hub — a comprehensive instructional media platform designed for AP and college-level physics students. This site brings together structured lessons, interactive simulations, and curated resources to support deep understanding of fundamental physics concepts.

Physics is the foundation of all natural science. Through this course, you will develop both conceptual understanding and quantitative problem-solving skills, preparing you for university studies in science, engineering, and mathematics.
